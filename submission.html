Paper Submission

Articles accepted at RASACC workshop will be included in the supplement to the DSN Proceedings (DSN-W volume) and made available on IEEE Xplore.

Submission requirements

  • description

    Paper types

    • Regular Papers present original, unpublished research with substantial new results and must be 8 pages total (including references)
    • Extended Abstracts foster discussion on recent or ongoing work, summarizing novel insights from a published paper or presenting preliminary results from ongoing research and must be 2 pages total (including references)
  • format_align_left

    Formatting rules

    • Submissions must follow the IEEE Computer Society 8.5″×11″ two-column camera-ready format.
    • Use a 10-point font on 12-point single-spaced leading.
    • Authors must use the official IEEE templates (updated 2024):
      • LaTeX Package (ZIP)
      • Word Template (DOC)
    • Each paper must be submitted as a single PDF file.
    • All fonts must be embedded in the PDF.
  • visibility_off Manuscripts must be anonymized for double-blind review.
  • library_books Papers not presented at the workshop will be excluded from the official proceedings.
