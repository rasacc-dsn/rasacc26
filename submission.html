Paper Submission

Articles accepted at RASACC workshop will be included in the supplement to the DSN Proceedings (DSN-W volume) and made available on IEEE Xplore.

Submission requirements

  • description The workshop welcomes three types of contribution:
  • Regular papers: 6 pages (plus 2 pages for references and appendix)
  • Extended asbtracts: 2 pages (including references)
  • visibility_off Manuscripts must be anonymized for double-blind review.
  • library_books Papers not presented will be excluded from the official proceedings.

Submission portal

Use easychair to submit your manuscript (https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=dsn_2026)
